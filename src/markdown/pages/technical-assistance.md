## OVERVIEW

Take the mystery out of securing state grant funding with an online ICJIA Your Recipe for Grant Success Course. This course will ease the application process for organizations seeking state grant funding. Participants will receive an overview of the ICJIA grant process, how to set up a budget and complete a program narrative, and what it means to comply with the Grant Accountability and Transparency Act (GATA).

Participants will learn:

- How to prepare to apply for a state grant via Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO).
- Where to look for available grants.
- What goes into a winning grant application.
- General application submission guidelines and requirements.

Participants also will learn the steps of the grant process, the importance of meeting GATA requirements, and how to remain in good standing with the state.

## COURSE DESIGN

In this online learning experience, participants learn at their own pace and review the modules as needed. Each module contains videos, presentations, and handouts to enhance the learning experience.

Participants will register via University of Illinois-Springfield Continuing and Professional Education Program (CAPE) using the Moodle learning platform.

Participants also may post questions and participate in live discussion forums. Online discussion forum date and times are posted monthly. Registration is required for live participant-lead question sessions.

## WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

The online workshop is open to anyone interested in applying for state grants. Agency directors, program managers, and others responsible for securing grant funding or seeking grants are encouraged to participate.
